Bonker Survival is a fast-paced arcade survival game where you control the iconic Bonker bomb character. Your mission is simple: stay alive for as long as possible while dodging relentless waves of enemies that grow faster and more aggressive with every passing second. Every run is a new challenge — how long can you last?

Built with Phaser.js and playable entirely in your browser with no download or sign-up required, Bonker Survival is perfect for quick sessions or marathon survival runs. The game features smooth 60fps gameplay, escalating difficulty, and a global leaderboard where logged-in players can compete for the top spot.

How to Play Bonker Survival

The goal is survival — dodge everything and keep moving. The arena fills with enemies that hunt you down, and the longer you survive, the more enemies spawn and the faster they move.

  • Move the Bonker character continuously — stopping even briefly can be fatal
  • Enemies spawn from the edges of the arena and home in on your position
  • The difficulty scales automatically: more enemies, higher speed, each wave
  • Collect power-ups that appear around the arena for temporary advantages
  • Use corners strategically, but never let yourself get trapped
  • Log in to save your high score and compete on the global leaderboard

Controls

Keyboard

  • WASD or Arrow Keys — Move the Bonker character
  • P or Escape — Pause the game
  • M — Toggle music on/off

Mobile / Touch

  • Virtual joystick — Tap and drag anywhere on screen to move
  • Works on all touch-enabled devices including iOS and Android browsers

Tips & Strategies

  • Keep moving at all times — a stationary Bonker is an easy target
  • Circle the arena — moving in wide loops can buy you breathing room
  • Prioritize power-ups — they can reverse a desperate situation instantly
  • Watch spawn points — enemies always enter from the arena edges, so avoid hugging the walls early
  • Survive the first 30 seconds — enemy density is lowest here; use it to learn patterns
  • The center is risky — enemies converge toward you, so diagonal paths through the center are dangerous

Features

  • Infinite survival gameplay with procedurally escalating difficulty
  • Global leaderboard — log in to save and compare scores
  • Smooth 60fps gameplay powered by Phaser.js
  • Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers
  • No download, no install, no sign-up required to play
  • Power-up system to keep gameplay dynamic and unpredictable
